Phillippines

Philippine is a tropical country located in Southeast Asia in the Western Pacific. The population is about 100 million. The capital of Philippines is Manila, which is located on the island Luzon. The official language of the Philippines is English and Filipino but there are 13 different types of dialects. Philippines are known for its beaches, natural structures such as the Chocolate Hills and Food. Fun fact about the Philippines is of the top 10 largest shopping mall in the world, three of them are found in the Philippines(Sm Megamall, Sm Northedsa, Sm Mall Of Asia).

Its 36,289 kilometers (22,549 mi) of coastline makes it the country with the 5th longest coastline in the world.
